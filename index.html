Become the best version of yourself

Depression? Anxiety? Doubts? We have been there and proudly been through it. Stress is an emotional or physical reaction to an event. Our body is designed to experience stress, in both negative and positive ways. However, if you experience negative stress for a longer period this can be bad for your health. Stress can have a negative impact on your work life and personal relationships.

Minimizing stress of daily life as much as possible is important for our overall health. That's because stress harms health and increases your risk of health conditions such as heart disease, anxiety disorders, and depression. It's important to understand that is not a mental health disorders, which require treatment from medical professionals. Here at Stress Escape we provide help to overcome your stress. We support you to become the best version of yourself. As stress triggers each individual differently, we aim to provide you customize support to get (back) to a healthy lifestyle and good mental-wellbeing.
